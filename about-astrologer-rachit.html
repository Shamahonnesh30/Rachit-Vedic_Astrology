Who Is Astrologer Rachit?

Born into a family with deep roots in the Vedic tradition, he began his formal study of Jyotish (Vedic astrology) under the guidance of his grandfather — a renowned astrologer in northern India — at the age of seven.

Over the following decades, Rachit Ji pursued rigorous academic study of Vedic astrology, earning his Jyotish Acharya qualification (the highest academic credential in Vedic astrology) alongside formal training in Vastu Shastra, Vedic numerology, palmistry (Samudrik Shastra) and tantric healing practices drawn from authenticated classical texts.

He established his UK practice over 15 years ago and has since become one of the most trusted names in Vedic astrology across the British-Indian, British-Pakistani and South Asian communities in London, Birmingham, Leicester, Manchester, Bradford and beyond — as well as serving a diverse clientele of British clients seeking genuine psychic guidance.

The Planetary Causes of Career Problems

In Vedic astrology, the 10th house governs career and public reputation. The 2nd house controls accumulated wealth. The 11th house rules income and gains. When these houses or their ruling planets are afflicted — by Saturn, Rahu, Ketu or Mars — the person experiences consistent obstacles to professional success regardless of how hard they work.

The most common planetary causes of career difficulties: Saturn (delays, obstacles, hard work without reward); Rahu (sudden unexpected losses, deception by partners); Ketu (lack of direction, spiritual disconnection from worldly goals); and a weak or afflicted Sun (lack of authority, difficulty commanding respect).
